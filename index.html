Indology.AI is a mission-driven initiative built on collaboration between AI engineers, classical scholars, linguists and passionate volunteers. There are many ways to contribute — no matter your background.

✦

Manuscript Transcription

Help digitise and transcribe Sanskrit, Tamil, Telugu and other classical manuscripts from our partner libraries and archives. No prior training required — guided onboarding provided.

अ

Translation & Review

Native or advanced speakers of Sanskrit, Pali, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu or Persian can help verify AI translations and contribute scholarly annotations to the knowledge base.

◈

Research Contribution

Academics and researchers can contribute essays, articles, primary source analyses and bibliography to the Indology.AI encyclopaedia — credited and peer-linked.

∞

Technology & Development

Developers, ML engineers and linguists can contribute to our open-source Sanskrit NLP toolkit, OCR models and knowledge graph infrastructure on GitHub.
